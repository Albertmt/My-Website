About Me

I am a fourth year Ph.D student in Mathematics at University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. My advisor is Professor B.Berndt, and I am also co-advised by Professor A.Zaharescu. My interests include analytic number theory. In particular, my current research is on distribution of zeros of combination of functions in Selberg class and their derivatives.
